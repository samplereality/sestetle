Sestetle
A sestet is either a 6-line poem or a 6-line stanza of a larger poem. Sestetle mashes sestets with Wordle, generating random poems using the primary constraint of Wordle, which is that each word is five letters long. The project is inspired by the occasionally meaningful list of wrong answers that made up my Wordle games.

Designed by Mark Sample (@samplereality)
